Crystals Collector

You will be given a random-number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal it will add a specific amount of points to your total score

You win the game by matching your total points to random number

You lose the game if your total points goes above the random number

Each time when the game starts, or you lose or win, the game will change the values of each crystal
